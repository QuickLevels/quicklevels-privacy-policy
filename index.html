PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 5, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how QuickLevels ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in relation to our Android mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. NO PERSONAL DATA COLLECTION BY US

We do not directly collect, process, store, or share any personal data from users of our App. Our App is designed to function without requiring:

  • User registration
  • Account creation
  • Personal information input
  • Contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

3. NO REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Our App can be used immediately upon installation without any registration process. You do not need to provide any personal information to access or use the App's features.

4. GOOGLE ANALYTICS

We use Google Analytics to understand user interests and improve our App. Google Analytics may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage patterns and app interactions
  • General location information (country/region level)
  • Technical data about app performance

This data collection is performed by Google's services, not directly by us. Google may use this information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

For more information about Google Analytics data collection, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our App may use additional Google services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase

These services may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

6. DATA RETENTION

Since we do not collect personal data directly, we do not retain personal information. Any data collected by Google Analytics is subject to Google's data retention policies.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@quicklevels.com

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete from our systems. For any data collected by Google services, please refer to Google's privacy controls and policies.
